People on the internet, television, and places such as
school and the workplace can be discourteous to each
other. For example, if you watch videos online, there is
always a troll in the comments section being extremely
rude to others or not expressing their opinion in a
well-mannered way. The way these trolls behave is disre-
spectful and they cause disharmony in the online com-
munity in which they are participating


I feel it is important to respect people, even people you
might not particularly like. Not being respectful can
show that you have poor character, are closed-minded.
